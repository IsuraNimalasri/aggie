Privacy Policy


Application Privacy Statement 


This privacy statement (“Privacy Statement”) applies to the treatment of personally identifiable information submitted by, or otherwise obtained from, social media users in connection with the associated application (“Iraq Election Monitoring application”, “Application”). The Application is provided by Creative Associates International (“Creative”). By using or otherwise accessing the Application, you acknowledge that you accept the practices and policies outlined in this Privacy Statement. 


The Iraq Election Monitoring application collects public information online from sources such as Twitter, Facebook, and RSS feeds so that a team of users can manually scan that information to identify and annotate notable events around the May 2018 election period in Iraq.


Personal Information


Creative will not collect personal information from Facebook or Twitter users. We receive and store information only from posts in public Facebook pages and groups and public Twitter posts deemed relevant for monitoring the Iraqi elections and related events. To fulfil the purpose of monitoring social media content related to the Iraq elections, we automatically collect only the following types of information from Facebook and Twitter users: username, date and time information, link, and post content. Creative will maintain collected data in the Iraq Election Monitoring application only for as long as needed for its business purpose. In this regard, the posts will be deleted in June 2018 after the election monitoring period. Information associated with posts collected through the Application (post content, link to post, and date of post) may be shared as part of the election monitoring process.


Use of Information


Creative uses the information described in this Privacy Statement (i) internally, to support election monitoring in Iraq, and (ii) as set forth below in the “Sharing of Personal Information” section below. 


Sharing of Personal Information


Creative will not receive or share any personal information from social media users with partners. 


Conditions of Use


Any possible dispute over privacy with Facebook or Twitter users whose information Creative receives using this Application is subject to this Privacy Statement and our Terms of Service, including limitations on damages, arbitration of disputes, and application of law. 


Use of Application by Children


The Application will only be used by election monitors associated with Creative. Creative does not knowingly collect social media content from children under the age of 13. There is a chance that children in this age range may post to the pages and groups included in the monitoring and content in the Application. However, through the Application Creative collects only public information from posts on public Facebook pages and groups, public Twitter posts, and public blogs and news sources. Creative does not collect personal information from users.
